RESONANCE is a touchable piece of electronic music.

Tap anywhere to create points, waves, and particles. As each wave reaches the points you placed, the world responds with light and sound. Tap near the beat to build SYNC, FLOW, TRANCE, and RESONANCE.

There are no enemies, scores, complicated controls, or wrong notes. Every touch is gently quantized to the music, so you can shape melodies and arpeggios without knowing music theory.

THE WORLD REMEMBERS

RESONANCE listens to the height, rhythm, and placement of your taps. Short phrases are remembered, played back one bar later, and transformed into a personal call and response.

GROW A COMPLETE TRACK

Each session develops over about one minute. The beat accelerates, visual connections emerge, and your melody and wave-contact patterns become part of the arrangement.

YOUR FINALE

At the end of a session, the music continues without interruption. Your remembered melody, arpeggio order, tap density, SYNC history, and point layout return in a two-part finale before the screen dissolves into white, silence, and a single new light.
