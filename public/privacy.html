Privacy Policy for RESONANCE

Effective date: July 20, 2026

RESONANCE: Tap the Sound is an offline audiovisual music application. This policy explains how the application handles information.

Information collection

RESONANCE does not require an account and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. Touch positions and musical choices are processed locally in the device's memory only for the current session.

Permissions and network access

The Android application does not request sensitive device permissions. The installed application does not require network access for its core experience.

Analytics, advertising, and purchases

RESONANCE contains no advertising, analytics SDK, tracking technology, in-app purchases, or third-party account system.

Children's privacy

RESONANCE is not specifically directed to children and knowingly collects no personal information from anyone.

Data retention and deletion

No personal data is retained. Session music state disappears when the session or application ends.

Changes to this policy

If the application's data practices change, this page and the Google Play Data Safety disclosure will be updated before those changes are released.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, use the project's public support page: GitHub Support.
